Rules of Tic Tac Toe

  • The objective is to place three of the same piece in a row, either:
    • Horizonal
    • Vertical
    • Diagonal

  • Players will place pieces alternating with X going first.

  • The game ends after a player wins by placing three of the same piece in a row OR the game board is full (9 moves) and results in cat's game.
